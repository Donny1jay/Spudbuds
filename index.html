About Spudbuds

At Spudbuds, quality starts at the root. We proudly use locally grown Cara potatoes, one of Egypt’s finest varieties, known for their perfect balance of fluffiness and structure. Their naturally starchy texture makes them ideal for baking, giving our jacket potatoes that crispy skin and soft, cloud-like center our customers love.

By sourcing our potatoes from trusted Egyptian farms, we support local agriculture and ensure every bite of our gourmet spuds is as fresh and flavorful as possible. It’s farm-to-food-truck goodness, baked to perfection.
